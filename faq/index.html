Frequently Asked Questions

What age range do you work with?

I work with all ages, from Preteens/Tweens (11 to 13) to Seniors (55+).

What are your accepted payment methods?

I accept Cash, Check, Discover, Mastercard, Visa, and FSA Debit Cards.

What is the average cost per session?

Average Cost per session ranges between $20 and $150.

What is your religious orientation?

While I am a Christian, I do not require my clients to have the same religious orientation as myself.

Do you use a financial sliding scale?

Yes! We can discuss payment options during your first visit.

What insurance plans do you accept?

Aetna, Anthem, BlueCross and BlueShield, Cigna, Coventry, Empire BlueCross, MHN, Optum, TRICARE, TriWest, and UnitedHealthcare.

*Verify your health insurance coverage when you arrange your first visit.*
