Qualifications

Master of Science

Awarded after completion of two years of graduate study. Many MS programs also require a thesis. Many therapists hold a master's degree, and many earn a master's "en route" to earning their doctoral degree.

Liscenced Mental Health Practitioner

Licensed Mental Health Practitioners (LMHP) work with clients and have an expanded ability to diagnose and treat severe mental illnesses.

Certified Eating Disorder Specialist

I am one of only three Certified Eating Disorder Specialists in the state of Nebraska. Of those three, I am the only practicing therapist with my CEDS Certification. My certification is through the International Association of Eating Disorder Professionals. I have been certified for more than twenty years.

Other Qualifications
